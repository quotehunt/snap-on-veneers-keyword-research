# Removable Veneers Buyer Intent Map

This document maps the common buyer journey for people researching snap-on veneers, pop-on veneers, clip-on veneers, and removable veneers.

The purpose is to show how searchers move from early problem awareness to purchase consideration.

## Overview

Most removable veneer buyers are not simply searching for a product. They are usually trying to solve a personal confidence problem.

Common starting points include:

- Stained teeth
- Missing teeth
- Gaps
- Chipped teeth
- Crooked teeth
- Worn teeth
- Dental anxiety
- High cost of cosmetic dentistry
- Upcoming events
- Feeling embarrassed to smile

Search behavior often reflects both practical and emotional concerns.
